My Approach

Successful trading is built on clarity, discipline, risk control, psychology, and consistency.

Knowing what you are doing, why you are doing it, and what your plan is before entering the market.

2. Discipline

Following your process, respecting your setup, and avoiding emotional or impulsive decisions.

3. Risk Management

Protecting capital, controlling drawdowns, and treating risk as a core part of trading success.

4. Psychology

Improving patience, emotional control, self-awareness, and confidence under market pressure.

5. Consistency

Building repeatable habits and focusing on long-term progress instead of short-term excitement.

Learning from mistakes, improving gradually, and approaching trading as a long-term skill.
